About Our Project

Making legal information accessible to everyone through the power of artificial intelligence.

Our Mission

The RTI & Legal AI Assistant project was developed with a mission to democratize access to legal information in India. We believe that understanding the law should not be limited to legal professionals but should be accessible to every citizen.

Our AI-powered system leverages advanced natural language processing technology to provide accurate, relevant, and easy-to-understand information about the Right to Information Act and other Indian laws, making legal knowledge more accessible to the general public.

Technology Behind the Project

Advanced AI Model

Powered by Google's Gemma 2B model, fine-tuned specifically for Indian legal context to provide accurate and contextually relevant information.

Comprehensive Legal Database

Our system is trained on a vast collection of Indian legal texts, including acts, court judgments, and legal commentaries.

Vector Search Technology

Uses advanced semantic search algorithms to find the most relevant legal information for each query.

FastAPI Backend

Built on a modern, high-performance web framework that ensures quick response times and reliability.

Important Disclaimer

The RTI & Legal AI Assistant is designed to provide information for educational purposes only. The information provided by our system should not be considered as legal advice.

For specific legal issues, we strongly recommend consulting with a qualified legal professional who can provide personalized advice based on your unique situation.

While we strive for accuracy, the AI system may occasionally provide incomplete or incorrect information. Always verify critical information with official sources.
